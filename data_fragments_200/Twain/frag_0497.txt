I lay he’d wait a pow’ful long time ’fo’ I _ast_ him. En mo’ en dat, I doan’ _want_ him to sleep wid me.” “Jim, don’t act so foolish. A prisoner’s _got_ to have some kind of a dumb pet, and if a rattlesnake hain’t ever been tried, why, there’s more glory to be gained in your being the first to ever try it than any other way you could ever think of to save your life.” “Why, Mars Tom, I doan’ _want_ no sich glory. Snake take ’n bite Jim’s chin off, den _whah_ is de glory? No, sah, I doan’ want no sich doin’s.” “Blame it, can’t you _try?_ I only _want_ you to try—you needn’t keep it up if it don’t work.” “But de trouble all _done_ ef de snake bite me while I’s a tryin’ him.
