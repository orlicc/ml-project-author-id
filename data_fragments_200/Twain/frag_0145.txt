Packard says, in a low voice: “All ready—shove off!” I couldn’t hardly hang on to the shutters, I was so weak. But Bill says: “Hold on—’d you go through him?” “No. Didn’t you?” “No. So he’s got his share o’ the cash yet.” “Well, then, come along; no use to take truck and leave money.” “Say, won’t he suspicion what we’re up to?” “Maybe he won’t. But we got to have it anyway. Come along.” So they got out and went in. The door slammed to because it was on the careened side; and in a half second I was in the boat, and Jim come tumbling after me. I out with my knife and cut the rope, and away we went! We didn’t touch an oar, and we didn’t speak nor whisper, nor hardly even breathe.
