He mightn’t ever see you—and if he did, maybe he’d never think anything.” “Well, if it’s pretty dark I reckon I’ll track him. I dono—I dono. I’ll try.” “You bet I’ll follow him, if it’s dark, Huck. Why, he might ’a’ found out he couldn’t get his revenge, and be going right after that money.” “It’s so, Tom, it’s so. I’ll foller him; I will, by jingoes!” “Now you’re _talking_!
