But answer me only jest this one more—now _don’t_ git mad; didn’t you have it in your mind to hook the money and hide it?” The duke never said nothing for a little bit; then he says: “Well, I don’t care if I _did_, I didn’t _do_ it, anyway. But you not only had it in mind to do it, but you _done_ it.” “I wisht I never die if I done it, duke, and that’s honest. I won’t say I warn’t _goin_’ to do it, because I _was;_ but you—I mean somebody—got in ahead o’ me.” “It’s a lie! You done it, and you got to _say_ you done it, or—” The king began to gurgle, and then he gasps out: “’Nough!—_I own up!_” I was very glad to hear him say that; it made me feel much more easier than what I was feeling before.
