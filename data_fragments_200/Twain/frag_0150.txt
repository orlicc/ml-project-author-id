Well, about an hour after dark we come along down in our trading-scow, and it was so dark we didn’t notice the wreck till we was right on it; and so _we_ saddle-baggsed; but all of us was saved but Bill Whipple—and oh, he _was_ the best cretur!—I most wish’t it had been me, I do.” “My George! It’s the beatenest thing I ever struck. And _then_ what did you all do?” “Well, we hollered and took on, but it’s so wide there we couldn’t make nobody hear.
