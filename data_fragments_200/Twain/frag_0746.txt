Sid would have thought. And Sid would have come and _done_ it, too. Tom, you’ll look back, some day, when it’s too late, and wish you’d cared a little more for me when it would have cost you so little.” “Now, auntie, you know I do care for you,” said Tom. “I’d know it better if you acted more like it.” “I wish now I’d thought,” said Tom, with a repentant tone; “but I dreamt about you, anyway. That’s something, ain’t it?” “It ain’t much—a cat does that much—but it’s better than nothing.
