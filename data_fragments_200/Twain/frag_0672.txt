Oh, _lordy_, lordy, lordy, I wisht I only had half your chance.” Tom choked off and whispered: “Look, Hucky, look! He’s got his _back_ to us!” Hucky looked, with joy in his heart. “Well, he has, by jingoes! Did he before?” “Yes, he did. But I, like a fool, never thought. Oh, this is bully, you know. _Now_ who can he mean?” The howling stopped. Tom pricked up his ears. “Sh! What’s that?” he whispered. “Sounds like—like hogs grunting. No—it’s somebody snoring, Tom.” “That _is_ it! Where ’bouts is it, Huck?” “I bleeve it’s down at ’tother end. Sounds so, anyway. Pap used to sleep there, sometimes, ’long with the hogs, but laws bless you, he just lifts things when _he_ snores.
