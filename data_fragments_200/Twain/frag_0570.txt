What do you keep _saying_ you will for? Why don’t you _do_ it? It’s because you’re afraid.” “I _ain’t_ afraid.” “You are.” “I ain’t.” “You are.” Another pause, and more eying and sidling around each other. Presently they were shoulder to shoulder. Tom said: “Get away from here!” “Go away yourself!” “I won’t.” “I won’t either.” So they stood, each with a foot placed at an angle as a brace, and both shoving with might and main, and glowering at each other with hate. But neither could get an advantage. After struggling till both were hot and flushed, each relaxed his strain with watchful caution, and Tom said: “You’re a coward and a pup. I’ll tell my big brother on you, and he can thrash you with his little finger, and I’ll make him do it, too.” “What do I care for your big brother?
