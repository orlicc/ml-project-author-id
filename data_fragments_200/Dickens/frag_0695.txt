I shall know what to do next; never fear.” The boy murmured a reply of intelligence: and hurried downstairs after his companions. “He has not peached so far,” said the Jew as he pursued his occupation. “If he means to blab us among his new friends, we may stop his mouth yet.” CHAPTER XIV.
