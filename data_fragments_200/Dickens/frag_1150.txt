He waited in a state of much suspense and uncertainty until the women, being committed for trial, went flaunting out; and then was quickly relieved by the appearance of another prisoner who he felt at once could be no other than the object of his visit. It was indeed Mr. Dawkins, who, shuffling into the office with the big coat sleeves tucked up as usual, his left hand in his pocket, and his hat in his right hand, preceded the jailer, with a rolling gait altogether indescribable, and, taking his place in the dock, requested in an audible voice to know what he was placed in that ’ere disgraceful sitivation for. “Hold your tongue, will you?” said the jailer. “I’m an Englishman, ain’t I?” rejoined the Dodger. “Where are my priwileges?” “You’ll get your privileges soon enough,” retorted the jailer, “and pepper with ’em.” “We’ll see wot the Secretary of State for
