What am I to dodge her for? Not to—” “Not to do anything, but to tell me where she goes, who she sees, and, if possible, what she says; to remember the street, if it is a street, or the house, if it is a house; and to bring me back all the information you can.” “What’ll yer give me?” asked Noah, setting down his cup, and looking his employer, eagerly, in the face. “If you do it well, a pound, my dear.
