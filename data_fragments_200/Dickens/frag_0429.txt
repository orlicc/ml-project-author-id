There have been a meeting to-night where he have been spoken of in the same shameful way. Stephen! The honestest lad, the truest lad, the best!’ Her indignation failed her, and she broke off sobbing. ‘I am very, very sorry,’ said Louisa. ‘Oh, young lady, young lady,’ returned Rachael, ‘I hope you may be, but I don’t know!
