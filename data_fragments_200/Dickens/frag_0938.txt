What account does he give of himself? Where did he come from? He didn’t drop out of the clouds, did he, master?” “Of course not,” replied the doctor, with a nervous glance at the two ladies. “I know his whole history: but we can talk about that presently. You would like, first, to see the place where the thieves made their attempt, I suppose?” “Certainly,” rejoined Mr. Blathers. “We had better inspect the premises first, and examine the servants afterwards.
