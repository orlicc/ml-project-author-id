Let this be a lesson to you, my man, or the law will overtake you yet. The boy is discharged. Clear the office!” “D—n me!” cried the old gentleman, bursting out with the rage he had kept down so long, “d—n me! I’ll—” “Clear the office!” said the magistrate. “Officers, do you hear? Clear the office!” The mandate was obeyed; and the indignant Mr. Brownlow was conveyed out, with the book in one hand, and the bamboo cane in the other: in a perfect phrenzy of rage and defiance. He reached the yard; and his passion vanished in a moment.
