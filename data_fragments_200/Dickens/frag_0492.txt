They were the only parts of his face that showed any life or expression, the pigment upon it was so thick. ‘You must be got to Liverpool, and sent abroad.’ ‘I suppose I must. I can’t be more miserable anywhere,’ whimpered the whelp, ‘than I have been here, ever since I can remember. That’s one thing.’ Mr. Gradgrind went to the door, and returned with Sleary, to whom he submitted the question, How to get this deplorable object away? ‘Why, I’ve been thinking of it, Thquire.
