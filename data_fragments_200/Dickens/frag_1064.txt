Come, come; say you wouldn’t.” “Well, then,” rejoined Mr. Sikes, “I wouldn’t. Why, damme, now, the girls’s whining again!” “It’s nothing,” said the girl, throwing herself into a chair. “Don’t you seem to mind me. It’ll soon be over.” “What’ll be over?” demanded Mr. Sikes in a savage voice. “What foolery are you up to, now, again?
