The robbery was committed by another boy. I saw it done; and I saw that this boy was perfectly amazed and stupified by it.” Having by this time recovered a little breath, the worthy book-stall keeper proceeded to relate, in a more coherent manner the exact circumstances of the robbery. “Why didn’t you come here before?” said Fang, after a pause. “I hadn’t a soul to mind the shop,” replied the man. “Everybody who could have helped me, had joined in the pursuit.
