If he was footsore and couldn’t walk, I sent him, in the letter he got, the money to ride, lest he should have none of his own to spare.’ ‘Let us hope that to-morrow will bring something better, Rachael. Come into the air!’ Her gentle hand adjusted Rachael’s shawl upon her shining black hair in the usual manner of her wearing it, and they went out. The night being fine, little knots of Hands were here and there lingering at street corners; but it was supper-time with the greater part of them, and there were but few people in the streets. ‘You’re not so hurried now, Rachael, and your hand is cooler.’ ‘I get better, dear, if I can only walk, and breathe a little fresh. ‘Times when I can’t, I turn weak and confused.’ ‘But you must not begin to fail, Rachael, for you may be wanted at any time to stand by Stephen.
