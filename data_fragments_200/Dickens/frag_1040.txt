Young Twist!” said Mr. Bumble; “I remember him, of course. There wasn’t a obstinater young rascal—” “It’s not of him I want to hear; I’ve heard enough of him,” said the stranger, stopping Mr. Bumble in the outset of a tirade on the subject of poor Oliver’s vices. “It’s of a woman; the hag that nursed his mother.
