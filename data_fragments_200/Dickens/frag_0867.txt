Pointing in the direction of Saffron Hill, he inquired whether any one was up yonder tonight. “At the Cripples?” inquired the man. The Jew nodded. “Let me see,” pursued the merchant, reflecting. “Yes, there’s some half-dozen of ’em gone in, that I knows.
