On glancing at the address, the parochial functionary observed that it contained no name. The stranger had not gone far, so he made after him to ask it. “What do you want?” cried the man, turning quickly round, as Bumble touched him on the arm. “Following me?” “Only to ask a question,” said the other, pointing to the scrap of paper. “What name am I to ask for?” “Monks!” rejoined the man; and strode hastily away.
