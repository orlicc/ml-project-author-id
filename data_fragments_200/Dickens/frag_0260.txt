And I also told you, if you remember, that I was up to the gold spoon look-out.’ ‘I were not up to ’t myseln, sir; I do assure yo.’ ‘Now it’s clear to me,’ said Mr. Bounderby, ‘that you are one of those chaps who have always got a grievance.
