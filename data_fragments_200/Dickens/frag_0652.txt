I—I would rather not press the case.” “Must go before the magistrate now, sir,” replied the man. “His worship will be disengaged in half a minute. Now, young gallows!” This was an invitation for Oliver to enter through a door which he unlocked as he spoke, and which led into a stone cell. Here he was searched; and nothing being found upon him, locked up. This cell was in shape and size something like an area cellar, only not so light. It was most intolerably dirty; for it was Monday morning; and it had been tenanted by six drunken people, who had been locked up, elsewhere, since Saturday night. But this is little.
