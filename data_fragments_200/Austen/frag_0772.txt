Then, he said, he would endeavour to see her in the course of this day.” “I am perfectly satisfied,” replied Emma, with the brightest smiles, “and most sincerely wish them happy.” “You are materially changed since we talked on this subject before.” “I hope so—for at that time I was a fool.” “And I am changed also; for I am now very willing to grant you all Harriet’s good qualities.
