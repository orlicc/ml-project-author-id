Do let me hear what she says.’” Emma’s politeness was at hand directly, to say, with smiling interest— “Have you heard from Miss Fairfax so lately? I am extremely happy. I hope she is well?” “Thank you. You are so kind!” replied the happily deceived aunt, while eagerly hunting for the letter.—“Oh! here it is.
