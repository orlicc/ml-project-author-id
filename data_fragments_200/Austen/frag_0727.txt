What is all this?” “She had engaged to go as governess to Mrs. Smallridge’s children—a dear friend of Mrs. Elton’s—a neighbour of Maple Grove; and, by the bye, I wonder how Mrs. Elton bears the disappointment?” “Say nothing, my dear Emma, while you oblige me to read—not even of Mrs. Elton.
