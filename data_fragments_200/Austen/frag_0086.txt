This letter, however, was written, and sealed, and sent. The business was finished, and Harriet safe. She was rather low all the evening, but Emma could allow for her amiable regrets, and sometimes relieved them by speaking of her own affection, sometimes by bringing forward the idea of Mr. Elton. “I shall never be invited to Abbey-Mill again,” was said in rather a sorrowful tone. “Nor, if you were, could I ever bear to part with you, my Harriet.
