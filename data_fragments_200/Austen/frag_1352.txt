And how impossible in others! But why should you wish to persuade me that I feel more than I acknowledge?” “That is a question which I hardly know how to answer. We all love to instruct, though we can teach only what is not worth knowing. Forgive me; and if you persist in indifference, do not make _me_ your confidante.” [Illustration: “Lizzy, my dear, I want to speak to you.” ] CHAPTER LV.
