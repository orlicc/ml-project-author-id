In a doleful voice Mrs. Bennet thus began the projected conversation:-- “Oh, Mr. Collins!” “My dear madam,” replied he, “let us be for ever silent on this point. Far be it from me,” he presently continued, in a voice that marked his displeasure, “to resent the behaviour of your daughter.
