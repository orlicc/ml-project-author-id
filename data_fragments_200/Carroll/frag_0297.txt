No doctors are enthusiastic; You are enthusiastic. You are not a doctor. 2. Dictionaries are useful; Useful books are valuable. Dictionaries are valuable. 3. No misers are unselfish; None but misers save egg-shells. No unselfish people save egg-shells. 4. Some epicures are ungenerous; All my uncles are generous. My uncles are not epicures. pg108 5.
