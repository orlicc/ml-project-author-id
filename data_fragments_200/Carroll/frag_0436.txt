I shall also extend the range of Syllogisms, by introducing Propositions containing alternatives (such as "Not-all x are y"), Propositions containing 3 or more Terms (such as "All ab are c", which, taken along with "Some bc' are d", would prove "Some d are a'"), &c. I shall also discuss Sorites containing Entities, and the _very_ puzzling subjects of Hypotheticals and Dilemmas.
