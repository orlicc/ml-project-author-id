(5) Ascertain the _Sign of Quantity_; (6) Arrange in the following order:-- Sign of Quantity, Subject, Copula, Predicate. [Let us work a few Examples, to illustrate these Rules. (1) "Some apples are not ripe." (1) The Subject is "apples." (2) The Verb is "are." (3) The Predicate is "not-ripe * * *." (As no Substantive is expressed, and we have not yet settled what the Univ. is to be, we are forced to leave a blank.) (4) Let Univ. be "fruit." (5) The Sign of Quantity is "some."
