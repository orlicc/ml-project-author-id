To begin with, we note that the phrase "none but the brave" is equivalent to "no _not_-brave." (1) The Subject has for its _Attribute_ "not-brave." But no _Substantive_ is supplied. So we express the Subject as "not-brave * * *." (2) The Verb is "deserve," for which we substitute the phrase "are deserving of". (3) The Predicate is "* * * deserving of the fair." (4) Let Univ. be "persons." (5) The Sign of Quantity is "no." (6) The Proposition now becomes "No | not-brave persons | are | persons deserving of the fair."
