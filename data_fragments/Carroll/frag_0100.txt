(2) "No xy' exist" = "No x are y'" = "No y' are x". (3) "No x'y exist" = "No x' are y" = "No y are x'". (4) "No x'y' exist" = "No x' are y'" = "No y' are x'". Let us take, next, the Proposition "All x are y".
